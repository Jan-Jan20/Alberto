About me

My name is Jan-Jan Alberto from Estanza Turaguis Legazpi City. I am 20 years old graduated at Daraga National Highschool and currently studying at Bicol University. I have 2 siblings and my father is woking as a truck driver also my mother is working as a housewife. My current hobby is to draw and play games with my friends to kill time and also for my relaxation. I started drawing when I was in grade school with the influence of the television programs like anime that is being broadcast before. I always invite my cousin to draw with me and back at that time my drawings are poorly drawn and I think it is the time that makes me interested in drawing. My parents are always angry because i am wasting the papers that they are using but I cant help but to draw because that is my passion. I know that I still have a lot of improvements and I am willing to learn all those things. Actually the course that I want to take is BSIT major in animation but there are things that I am considering, number 1 is the travel costs from my house to the campus which is from legazpi to polangui. The second is I dont have the right device for animation which requires high specs with a high performance for rendering and storing stuffs and programs. There's no other choice but to continue the course that I am taking right now because it aligns with my long-term goals and aspirations. Despite the challenges and obstacles that may arise along the way, I firmly believe that perseverance and dedication will lead me to success. This course not only provides valuable knowledge and skills but also serves as a stepping stone towards a fulfilling career or personal growth. By staying committed and focused, I can maximize the opportunities presented by this course and make the most of my potential. While it may be tempting to consider alternative paths, I am confident that staying on this course is the best decision for my overall development and future prospects. We dont know what future may take us but the thing is we should love the things we have right now, some says that life is short and I believe in that saying. The one thing that I learned about life is try to be simple, apprecite the beauty, and to enjoy every moment cause that moment may not come back tommorrow, the coming days, months, or forever.
